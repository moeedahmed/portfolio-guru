📋 Kaizen Portfolio Form Mapper

Extracts all form types and their field structures from your Kaizen ePortfolio.
This helps build an AI tool that auto-fills portfolio forms for doctors.

✅ This is read-only. It reads form names and field labels from pages you can already see. It does not modify anything, submit any forms, or access your personal data/submissions.

1 Log into your Kaizen portfolio

Go to eportfolio.rcem.ac.uk and log in as normal. Wait for the dashboard to load.

2 Go to "Add an activity"

Click "Add an activity" in the menu, or go directly to kaizenep.com/events/new. Wait for the list of forms to appear.

3 Open the browser console

  • Chrome/Edge: Press F12 then click Console tab
  • Mac: Press Cmd + Option + J
  • Firefox: Press F12 then click Console tab

4 Copy and paste this script

Click the box below to select all, then Ctrl+C / Cmd+C to copy. Paste it into the console and press Enter.

What happens when you run it:
  • A blue box appears in the top-right showing progress
  • The script reads each form's fields (takes 1-2 minutes)
  • When done, the box turns green and the result is on your clipboard
  • Paste it into WhatsApp/Telegram and send it back

5 Send the result back

When the green "Done!" message appears, open any messaging app and press Ctrl+V / Cmd+V to paste. Send it to whoever asked you to do this.

⚠️ Note: Some forms may show "Could not read form" — this happens when the form page blocks embedding. The form name and ID are still captured; only the field details are missing for those. This is normal.
