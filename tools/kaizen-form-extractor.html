📋 Kaizen Form Extractor

This tool extracts all form types and their IDs from your Kaizen ePortfolio. It reads only — it doesn't change anything in your portfolio.

Step 1: Log into Kaizen

Go to eportfolio.rcem.ac.uk and log in as normal.

Step 2: Go to the "New Activity" page

Click "Add an activity" (or navigate to kaizenep.com/events/new).

Step 3: Open your browser console

Press F12 (or Cmd+Option+J on Mac), then click the Console tab.

Step 4: Paste this script and press Enter

This script reads the form list on the page and copies it to your clipboard. It does not modify anything.

Step 5: Send the result

After running the script, it will say "Copied X forms to clipboard!"

Open any messaging app and press Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V) to paste, then send it to Moeed.

⚠️ This only reads publicly visible form names and IDs from the page. It does not access your personal data, submissions, or credentials.
